From our first letter in 1979, announcing the vision of this community, to our current letter we always thank you, our faithful readers, for being partners with us in the work that God has called us to.

 

If you join our mailing list you will only receive the newsletters and we will not share your address with anyone.  

 

The Jubilee Newsletter is mailed out four times a year and written by various members of our community. You can subscribe to recieve it by email or in the mail here.
